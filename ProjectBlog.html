Stardust

Introducing Stardust, a highly customizable habit-tracking app meticulously crafted to promote and maintain good habits in users. Built with the robust and flexible Flutter framework and Dart language, Stardust offers an intuitive and delightful user experience, ensuring seamless performance across multiple platforms. With its extensive theme options, users can personalize their interface to reflect their style and preferences, making habit tracking not just a routine but a delightful daily experience.

At the heart of Stardust is the integration of Firebase Cloud support, offering reliable and secure data management. Your progress, habits, and personal settings are safely stored in the cloud, ensuring your data remains intact and accessible across your devices. Whether you're switching phones or syncing between multiple devices, Stardust has you covered.

Stardust isn't just an app; it's a lifestyle companion. It encourages users to cultivate and maintain good habits through its user-friendly design and motivational insights. With Stardust, you're not alone in your journey to self-improvement. The app's smart tracking features and insightful statistics make habit formation enjoyable and achievable.

Embrace the magic of habit formation with Stardust, where every step towards your goal is a step towards a better you. This project launches February 28th, stay tuned for multiplatform support!

3D Segmenting AI

Delving into the cutting-edge realm of AI-driven image processing, my research project embarks on an ambitious journey to bridge the dimensional gap between 2D imagery and 3D model generation. At the core of this innovative endeavor lies the adaptation and enhancement of the open-source technology, Segment Anything, a tool celebrated for its proficiency in discerning and isolating distinct elements within two-dimensional images with remarkable precision.

This project is not merely a technological venture but a bold exploration into the depths of AI's potential. By harnessing the capabilities of Segment Anything, our team is dedicated to extending its functionality beyond the confines of 2D realms, venturing into the intricate and uncharted territories of 3D model generation. The goal is to develop a robust system capable of interpreting and transforming 2D visual data into detailed, three-dimensional representations

The implications of this research are profound. In fields ranging from medical imaging to architectural design, the ability to accurately convert 2D images into 3D models stands to revolutionize industry practices, offering enhanced visualization, intricate detailing, and a new dimension of analytical capability.

This project is still in its beginning phases as far as research goes, but a repository is in progress. I am currently working on perfecting the creation of 3D meshes based on 2D images.

Shonto Solar

I was previously involved in the development of solar panels through Arizona State University's Engineers Without Borders program in collaboration with EPICS (Engineering Projects in Community Service) and the Navajo Nation's Shonto Chapter. I was specifically working on the solar panel division in 2021-2022. Programming in Python on visual displays and encorporating information from the user manual so the members of the Shonto Chapters could troubleshoot their own solar panels instead of having to wait potentially days or a week for a member of the team to drive over and troubleshoot.
